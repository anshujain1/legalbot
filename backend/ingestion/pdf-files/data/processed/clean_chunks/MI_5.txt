16th May 2025 | Aizawl, Mizoram As India strides towards its vision of Viksit Bharat 2047, digital transformation stands as the cornerstone of Governance, Innovation, and Sustainable Development. We are delighted to announce the upcoming 17th Edition of Digital Transformation Conclave scheduled to take place on the 16th of May in Aizawl, Mizoram. Hosted by Department of Information & Communication Technology, Government of Mizoram and Organized by GovConnect (an ilouge Media initiative), The theme for the Conclave is "Weaving The Digital Future for Viksit Bharat 2047." The Digital Transformation Conclave in Mizoram brings together Key Government Decision - Makers, Industry Leaders, and Technology Experts from across the Country to explore cutting-edge solutions shaping India’s digital governance landscape. Nestled in the Scenic Northeast, Mizoram is rapidly emerging as a hub for digital innovation in governance, infrastructure, and citizen services. With a proactive government and increasing digital adoption, Mizoram offers a high-impact opportunity for industry players to engage with policymakers and unlock new business potential. Mizoram, along with the broader Northeast, presents immense opportunities for digital infrastructure, cybersecurity, AI-driven governance, cloud computing, and connectivity solutions.
