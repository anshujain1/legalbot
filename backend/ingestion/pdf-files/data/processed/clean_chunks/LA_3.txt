Lakshadweep administrator announces plan for smart classrooms in all schools Lakshadweep Administrator Praful Patel on Monday said all classrooms in schools in the islands will be turned into smart classrooms. Lakshadweep Administrator, Praful Patel, announced plans to transform all classrooms in schools across the islands into smart classrooms. The announcement came during the vibrant 'Praveshnolsavam' event held to welcome new students. Patel also pledged to provide electronic tablets to Lakshadweep domicile students pursuing higher education. The event, held at Government Junior Basic School North, was officially inaugurated by Administrator Praful Patel. The ceremony commenced with a guard of honour by the school's NCC and band team. In his address to the students, Patel assured them of a bright educational future in Lakshadweep. He emphasised a zero-tolerance policy towards drug peddlers and traffickers, assuring that strict measures would be taken. Patel also promised modern infrastructure in all 48 schools under the administration of the Union Territory of Lakshadweep. To promote holistic development, Patel pledged maximum support for sports activities. He announced that meritorious students in Class 10 and Plus Two examinations would be recognised and rewarded with cash prizes. During interactions with the teaching community, Patel stressed the importance of discipline, maintaining a dress code, and ensuring the upkeep of school premises. Patel suggested celebrating students' birthdays in classrooms to foster a homely atmosphere. He also proposed regular visits to students' homes and interactions with parents to establish a healthy rapport between parents and teachers. The administrator believed that such initiatives

would strengthen the school community. A SUCCESSFUL START TO THE ACADEMIC YEAR During the inaugural ceremony, Patel interacted with newly admitted Class 1 students, numbering over 900 for this academic year. He inspected classrooms and the school premises, expressing appreciation for the newly introduced smart classrooms. The event featured music, cultural performances, and the distribution of gifts and sweets to welcome the new students. Notably, all students and members of the School Management Committee (SMC) were present at the Praveshnolsavam event. With the commitment to transform classrooms into smart learning spaces, Lakshadweep schools are poised for an enhanced educational experience and a brighter future for its students.
