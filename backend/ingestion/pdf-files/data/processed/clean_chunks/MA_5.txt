05.01.2025- Governor presides over the Artificial Intelligence in Media Innovation Conference in Pune Governor presides over the Artificial Intelligence in Media Innovation Conference in Pune Maharashtra Governor C.P. Radhakrishnan presided over the Artificial Intelligence in Media Innovation Conference in Pune. The Conference was organized by the Marathi Patrakar Sangh Pune. Editor of the Pudhari Group Smita Yogesh Jadhav, Yadu Joshi of Dainik Lokmat, Executive Editor of Daily Sakal Sheetal Pawar, Editor of News 18 Lokmat Vilas Badhe were given the ‘Editor Vratasta Puraskar’. Chairman of the Pudhari Group Yogesh Jadhav, Group Editor of Dainik Lokmat Vijay Baviskar, Founder of Ruperi Kinar Foundation Kalpana Javadekar, State President of Marathi Patrakar Sangh Kiran Joshi were present. Special awards were given to Mrs. Kalpana Javadekar of Ruperi Kinar and Nilesh Khedekar of Edutech.
