Shillong: The Meghalaya government is set to integrate robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and emerging technologies into the school curriculum as part of an ongoing revision of the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) and the State Curriculum Framework (SCF). Chief Secretary DP Wahlang confirmed that alongside these updates, students will gain exposure to AI, robotics, the Internet of Things (IoT), and new language learning programmes. Talking to Mediapersons, the Chief Secretary said, "Yes, definitely. The school curriculum is being revised. Along with the National Curriculum Framework (NCF), the State Curriculum Framework (SCF) is also undergoing revision. With these updates, new fields such as robotics, AI, the Internet of Things (IoT), and new language learning will be introduced. All these will be part of the curriculum, and I am sure students will benefit." Emphasizing the need for a modern and dynamic education system, Wahlang added, "We are in touch with NCERT, and we will incorporate whatever NCERT includes in the curriculum.
