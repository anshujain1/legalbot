In a significant stride towards bolstering security, the Jammu and Kashmir Police's District Police Ramban, under the leadership of SSP Ramban Sh. Anuj Kumar, IPS, has activated a cutting-edge AI-based facial Recognition System at Navyuga Checking Plaza, Banihal. This plaza falls under the jurisdiction of the Jawahar Tunnel Police Station Banihal, showcasing the police's proactive approach to ensuring public safety. This device, with its unparalleled precision, is designed to locate and monitor individuals involved in criminal activities, such as drug dealers, terrorists, OGWs, absconders, history sheeters, and thieves. The system's deployment underscores the region's unwavering commitment to public safety and its successful fight against crime. SSP Ramban has highlighted the importance of the AI-based facial recognition system in quickly identifying and capturing people with criminal records. The technology's capacity to recognize a variety of threats, from local crime to terrorism, is anticipated to be essential to upholding law and order in the community. Among the essential components of smart policing programs is the AI-based facial Recognition System. Through the use of this cutting-edge technology, law enforcement organizations hope to improve not just the efficiency with which they track down offenders but also contribute to the larger framework of proactive and intelligent policing. This action aligns with the dedication to using creative approaches to protect public safety and preserve the values of smart, technologically advanced law enforcement.
